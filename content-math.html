Math 7.NS.A.1 Unit 1 — Number Sense
Rational Numbers — Adding and Subtracting
🎬 Hook

You've already been using negative numbers your whole life — you just didn't know it. Every time someone owes money, checks the temperature in winter, or talks about sea level, they're doing math with negatives. Watch this 90-second clip to see where negative numbers show up in the real world.

✅ I Can…

I can add and subtract positive and negative rational numbers and explain why subtraction is the same as adding the opposite.

🎯 I'll Know I've Got It When…

I can solve problems with negative numbers without a calculator and explain my thinking out loud — no peeking at notes.
